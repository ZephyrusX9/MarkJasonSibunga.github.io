Mobile Loan Tracking System For Students in PHINMA University of Pangasinan

The use of Mobile Loan Tracking System for Students of PHINMA University of Pangasinan is to increase the Finance Department's efficiency and effectiveness in loan collecting. This system will cut down on the time it takes to track down kids who owe money to the school. The outstanding balance amount that a student owes is automatically shown by the system. The website will offer cloud-based transactions for lending applications, as well as a comprehensive view of the loan's associated paperwork.
